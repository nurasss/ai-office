# Pilot SLA

## Source status

This file contains pilot defaults. Replace it with official company SLA from
Confluence, Jira workflow settings or team regulations.

## Defaults

| Task class | Target first response | Target first draft | Owner |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Routing | 5 minutes | 15 minutes | PMO |
| L1 support | 2 hours | 4 hours | Support |
| Short content | 4 hours | 1 business day | Copywriter |
| Analytics request | 4 hours | 1 business day | Data Analyst |
| Code review | 4 hours | 1 business day | Developer |
| Market memo | 1 business day | 2 business days | Strategist |
| Invoice check | 2 hours | 4 hours | Accountant |

## SLA breach handling

- PMO marks the task as blocked or overdue.
- PMO identifies the missing source, access or decision.
- PMO escalates to a human owner when the agent cannot proceed safely.
