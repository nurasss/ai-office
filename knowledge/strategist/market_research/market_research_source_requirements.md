# Market research source requirements

## Required metadata

Every market research file must include:

- source title;
- author or organization;
- publication date;
- access date;
- geography;
- segment;
- whether data is primary research, secondary research or internal assumption.

## Accepted source types

- Internal investor presentations.
- Product roadmap documents.
- Customer discovery summaries.
- Analyst reports with usage rights.
- Industry media articles with source date.
- Public competitor pages saved with access date.

## Required analysis separation

Strategist must separate:

- facts from sources;
- internal assumptions;
- interpretation;
- risks and limitations;
- recommended next steps.

## Missing-source answer

If market size, competitor pricing or macroeconomic data is not loaded, the
answer must say that dated research is missing and should not present guessed
numbers.
