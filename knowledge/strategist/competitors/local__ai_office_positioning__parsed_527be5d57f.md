# AI Office positioning

## Category

AI Office is positioned as a controlled multi-agent workplace assistant, not a
single general-purpose chatbot.

## Differentiators

- Seven role-specific agents with separate prompts and tools.
- PMO entry point for routing and decomposition.
- Isolated RAG namespaces to reduce context contamination.
- Route-only mode for cheap inspection.
- Human review remains part of the operating model.
