# Editorial policy for AI Office

## Core voice

AI Office writes with calm technological optimism. We explain how the system
reduces routine and speeds up first results without promising that AI replaces
people or makes decisions alone.

## Addressing the reader

Default form: neutral business language. Use direct, clear phrasing. If the
channel has a known rule, follow that channel's rule from the approved source.

## What to emphasize

- AI Office is a controlled system of specialized AI employees.
- Each agent has a role, tools, model choice and quality rules.
- PMO routes work instead of letting one generic chat answer everything.
- Human review remains part of the process.
- Missing data is called out explicitly.

## Stop words and weak phrases

Avoid:

- революционный без proof;
- уникальный without a specific differentiator;
- автоматизирует все;
- заменяет сотрудников;
- гарантирует результат;
- максимально эффективный;
- в целях повышения;
- осуществляем;
- ввиду того что.

## Formatting rules

- Use short paragraphs.
- Prefer concrete verbs.
- Put the main value in the first two sentences.
- For social posts, keep one clear CTA.
- For emails, include subject and preheader.
- For landing copy, separate headline, support text and CTA.

## Fact-check rule

If a claim involves price, model name, KPI, legal detail, tax rate, benchmark or
market size, Copywriter must use verified input from PMO or mark the claim as
requiring fact-check.
