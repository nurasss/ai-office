# AI Office reference content

## Reference post: launch announcement

We launched AI Office: a digital office with seven specialized AI employees.

PMO routes tasks, the analyst checks metrics, the developer reviews code, the
copywriter prepares drafts, support handles first-line questions, the strategist
structures market research, and the accountant checks financial documents.

The important part is control. Each agent sees only its own knowledge base and
the shared corporate context, so work does not turn into one generic chat.

Next step: send a real task through PMO and compare how much faster the first
draft appears.

## Reference email

Subject: AI Office pilot is ready for real tasks

Preheader: Seven focused AI agents now work through one PMO entry point.

Hello,

We prepared the AI Office pilot for practical tasks. The system now separates
work between seven AI employees: PMO, analyst, developer, copywriter, support,
strategist and accountant.

The main rule is knowledge isolation. Each agent receives only the shared
corporate context and its own role-specific documents. This keeps content,
finance, support, analytics and engineering from blending into one unreliable
answer.

Please send the first pilot tasks through PMO. Mark anything that needs human
approval, source verification or access to internal systems.
